What is OASIS?

OASIS is my attempt to build a real-life JARVIS — one open-source AI that runs your home and, if you build it, a working Iron Man suit. At the center is DAWN, a natural-voice assistant wrapped in sensors, a heads-up display, and wireless hardware that all runs on machines you own.

I built every piece of the software myself, from the ground up. It runs on NVIDIA Jetson and Raspberry Pi, and the whole stack is GPLv3 — from the voice assistant to the HUD renderer to the repulsor firmware.

The flagship

Meet DAWN — your own JARVIS/FRIDAY

One natural-voice AI that listens, reasons, calls tools, and remembers you — running entirely on hardware you own. It runs your whole home — smart devices, calendar, music, and more — and comes with you into the OASIS suit when you wear it. No cloud account, no subscription, nothing leaving your house. The closest thing to a real JARVIS or FRIDAY that actually exists.

  • 20+ built-in tools — calendar, email, smart home, music, memory, messaging, and more
  • Remembers you — the people and preferences that matter, across every conversation
  • In every room — Raspberry Pi & ESP32 voice satellites
  • Cloud-optional — runs fully offline on a single Jetson, or connect cloud services if you prefer

DAWN is the brain. Here's the body.

Six more open components — HUD, sensors, telemetry, comms, hand, and chassis. Every piece (DAWN included) is its own independent project: each runs perfectly well stand-alone, and they combine into a full suit when you bring them together.

MIRAGE

Heads-Up Display

Dual-display AR with a live camera passthrough plus gauges, compass, maps, and up to 16 switchable HUD screens. Hardware H.264 encoding for recording and live streaming.

Learn more STAT

Telemetry Daemon

Host-system telemetry for the machine running DAWN — CPU/GPU load and temperature, memory, power draw, and battery. Publishes over MQTT; Python GUI for real-time visualization.

Learn more ECHO

Cellular Daemon

SIM7600G-H modem daemon. Routes phone calls and SMS to DAWN with multi-segment text support and inbound ringtone notifications.

Learn more AURA

Helmet Sensors

ESP32-S3 sensor suite: 9-DOF IMU, GPS, CO2, air quality, temperature, and humidity. FreeRTOS with servo-controlled faceplate.

Learn more SPARK

Armor & Repulsors

The armor's gauntlet firmware — gesture control, repulsor LED effects, wireless, and audio-synced sound — plus monitoring of the armor's own battery and temperature, reported to the HUD.

Learn more BEACON

CAD Library

Full 3D printable parts library for the suit build. Enclosures, mounts, display chassis, and structural components. Free STL downloads.
